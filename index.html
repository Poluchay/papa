The camping table with a difference

The Car Bar was born from being frustrated at the size and faff of typical camping gear. We wanted something that was small enough to leave in the car at all times for those impromptu trips, but practical enough to use for camping. It also needed to be so quick and easy to set up that we’d never put off using it.
